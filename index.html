Hi! I am Munachiso, I'm a passionate engineer, analyst, and business nerd!

I studied computer science (CS) at my undergraduate alma mater, NYU, and proceeded to delve into the world of machine learning and statistics, obtaining my masters at the Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence.

One of my favourite MSc projects was auto-hyperparameter tuning [Project Page]. I wrote all the baseline code both on the algorithm side (think Bayesian Optimisation, Stats), and on the ML systems side (think Docker, Kubernetes, Argo Workflows).

I contributed to the SautiDB Project, a project I originally coined and proposed to our team, supported by a research grant from DL Indaba, to use ML in improving e-learning for African students.

One of my first deep learning projects was a mobile app that interprets sign language spelling into plain alphabet.
