Hi! I am Munachiso. I recently got my undergraduate degree at NYU Abu Dhabi. I enjoy doing research, and I blog about my favourite projects.

As an undergraduate, I did my thesis with the NYU MMVC Lab on Semantic Segmentation of Satellite Images, under professor Yi Fang.

I am currently working with the Clinical AI Lab with professor Farah Shahmout on ChestXray analysis using Deep Learning.

During a five day break from school in 2019, I and two friends created a mobile application that interprets Sing Language Finger Spelling using Deep Learning. We demonstrated that using our prototype, you can understand sign language with just a mobile phone.
